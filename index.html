Our Story

This Must Be The Place

Nestled in the heart of Gaborone's Main Mall, Main Deck Restaurant & Bar is where great food meets an unforgettable vibe. From the glow of our neon sign to the sizzle of a perfectly cooked T-bone, every detail is crafted to make you feel something.

We serve Botswana-proud food — hearty pot dishes, flame-kissed steaks, loaded burgers carrying the Botswana flag, fresh pasta, and drinks that hit right. Whether it's breakfast, a long lunch, or a Friday night out — the Deck has a seat for you.
